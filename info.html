FocuxTube Guide

Learn how to browse YouTube distraction-free

Hide Thumbnails

Completely removes all video thumbnails from your YouTube feed, helping you make more conscious viewing decisions.

Blur Thumbnails

Applies a blur effect to thumbnails, reducing visual noise while maintaining context about video content.

Solid Color Mode

Replaces thumbnails with a neutral color, creating a clean and distraction-free browsing experience.

Distraction-Free Mode

Removes related videos, comments, and other distracting elements to help you focus on the current video.
